What We Do?

DSC or Developers Student Club is a program under Google Developers that targets undergraduate students.

It's main focus is to help the students identify the current technologies used in the industry and educate them for the same so that they become excellent developers.

"We are a bunch of enthusiastic technocrats who took the responsibility of delivering this required knowledge to you."
