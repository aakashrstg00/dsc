"The Season begins"

The first ever challenge in the series is designed keeping in mind the beginners. The challenge is simple and easy to implement.


PRIZE POOL - ₹2000


Themes:

The themes are decided by the problem statements, so currently there are 3 themes -

  • College Life
  • Environment
  • Daily Life

What the participants have to do:

Each team must create a presentation of max 8 slides, consisting of the problem, the pre-existing solution faults etc. and your solution.

These are the themes according to which you can find the current problems and come up with a solution. In case you aren't able to think of any, you can find a solution for one of the problem statements given below:

  1. Design a solution to monitor solar panels over a large area with central control and monitoring platform.
  2. Build a service to help students do mock interviews (like interviewing.io). Keep in mind the technical design; incentive for students; incentive for interviewers etc.
  3. Design a platform to manage multiple social media accounts(like Hootsuite). Keep in mind the technical design; subscriptions; monetizations etc.
  4. Suggest a solution to reduce traffic congestion or increase parking efficiency.
  5. Delhi is one of the most polluted cities. Suggest a solution to reduce pollution.
  6. Suggest a solution to tackle the problems associated with mass bunks and missing classes in general.
  7. Many a time your friends drop a reference while talking and you don't know what it means, making you feel left out of the conversation. Suggest a solution to tackle this problem.

SELECTION PROCESS

PHASE I:
The students will form a team among themselves and create a presentation on the problem within 5 days.
The presentation will be submitted via Google forms.
The judges will start evaluating the presentations as soon as they receive them. To reduce the burden on the judging team, weâ€™ll allow early submissions.
After the deadlines, the judges will be given two days extra to wrap up and update the leaderboards.
The top 10 teams will then proceed to the next round.

PHASE II:
The teams that passed phase I will undergo QnA round where they will be asked to pitch their idea and will undergo questioning on many perspectives such as business models, market strategy, any weaknesses etc.
The top 10 will be declared winners and given prizes.
